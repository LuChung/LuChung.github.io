MY DESIGN CONCEPT

Assistance home robot for family

This is the app interface for movable home monitoring robot for Techgiant, Inc. We wanted to build a commercially assistance home robot which provided educational fun for kids, and helped seniors to enjoy a connected life with their distant family. I organized the features of voice-controlled interfaces and touchscreen interfaces, so that family members of all ages could feel comfortable and joyful to control robot easily.

VISUAL IDENTITY

Logo / Icon / Color Palette / Font Style

The concept of this Robelf logo is inspired by its appearance which with a big screen, and a camera lens on the center head. I also designed the series of icons for the application functions.

The main color using with a large color block, like a button, is derived originally from the core Robelf Turquoise, but evolved into a much brighter and more saturated color. And the grays, variations colors are useful for form field validation, alerts, warnings, and pats on the back. Optimized for color blindness.

FLAT ELEMENTS

Button / List / Status

I created this this flat design of Robeld app interfaces, the style emphasizing minimum use of stylistic elements that give the illusion of three dimensions, such as the use of drop shadows, gradients or textures.
